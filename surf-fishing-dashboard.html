NWS AMZ158 — Official

NOAA 8656483 — Official
All tide times and heights from NOAA Tides & Currents station 8656483 (Beaufort / Morehead City), the closest official reference station to Atlantic Beach. Astronomically calculated, accurate to within minutes. Source: tidesandcurrents.noaa.gov
tides4fishing — Solunar
Solunar activity ratings and tidal coefficients from tides4fishing.com (Atlantic Beach page). Used only for solunar scoring — lunar-cycle math is reliable here, but this source is not used for wind or surf since it has previously disagreed with the official NWS forecast. Source: tides4fishing.com/us/north-carolina/atlantic-beach
