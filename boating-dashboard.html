Source Legend & Scoring Guide

NWS — Official
Wind, sound conditions, tides (Sun), and advisories pulled directly from the National Weather Service marine forecast (AMZ158/AMZ100) and Bogue Inlet tide tables. Sound ratings use only the explicit "adjacent sounds and rivers" language — ocean seas are not used.
est. — Estimated
Mon–Thu tide times are projected by advancing ~50 min/day from the NWS Sunday anchor (12:38 AM / 1:17 PM highs). Wed–Thu wind/sound are generalized from the source's brief "Tue night through Thu" summary — treat as low-confidence. Solunar data was not present on the source page.
Wind Scoring
<10 kt = 5/5 · 10–15 = 4/5 · 15–20 = 3/5
20–25 = 2/5 · >25 or gusts 30+ = 1/5
(Gusts pull the score down a tier)
Sound Scoring
Flat = 5/5 · Light chop = 4/5 · Moderate chop = 3/5
Choppy = 2/5 · Rough / Very rough = 1/5
Tide Scoring
High tide 8 AM–4 PM = 5/5 ideal · Just outside window = 4/5 · Well outside = 3/5 or lower
Overall Score
Average of Wind, Sound, and Tide. Active Small Craft Advisory or thunderstorm risk caps the day's overall score (SCA → 2/5 max, tstm → 3/5 max).
